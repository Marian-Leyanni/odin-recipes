Baked pancakes with blueberries and bananas

A super easy recipe for making the sweetest pancakes

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Start by sifting the dry ingredients together
  2. Next, make a well, add the milk, butter, and egg, and stir together
  3. Heat a pan, scoop some of the mixture, and spread it onto the pan, till all is gone
  4. Cook on low heat for a minute or two, then flip to other side
  5. Cook till both sides are brown, then plate
