Refreshing Mango Puree

With this recipe, you'll get a glass of all-natural fruit juice out of a few mangoes

Ingredients

  • Sweet ripened mangoes
  • Sugar/Honey
  • Mint leaves
  • Orange juice/Coconut Water

Steps

  1. Carefully wash and peel the mangoes
  2. Cut up the inner flesh, and scoop into blender
  3. Add 1 or 2 tablespoons of sugar or honey to the blender
  4. If preferred, wash and add some mint leaves
  5. Add some orange juice, coconut water or even plain water to lighten the mixture
  6. Finally, blend till smooth and serve.
  7. If the mixture remains thick, you can add some more liquid
