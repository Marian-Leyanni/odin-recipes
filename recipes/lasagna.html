Lasagna

Make the best Lasagnas with this recipe

Making great lasagna comes down to prepping your ingredients well, propely layering, then baking.

No tedious process. Just good vibes and good food.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Spices

Steps to take

  1. Cook and drain your grond beef
  2. Add together your cheese, eggs, and spices
  3. Arrange the lasagna according to your preference
  4. Cover and bake for 45 minutes
  5. Next, uncover and leave it baking an extra 10 minutes
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
