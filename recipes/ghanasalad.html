Ghanaian Salad

Fresh Vegetable Ghanaian Salad

Follow this recipe to make your own bowl

You'll come to understand why Ghanaian Salad is all the rave

Ingredients

  • Lettuce of about 16 ounces
  • 2 Cucumbers
  • 2 medium sized Carrots
  • 1 large Onion
  • Can of Baked Beans
  • 2 large Tomatoes
  • 3 hard-boiled Eggs
  • 1 large avocado
  • Salad Cream
  • Tomato Ketchup, if preferred

Steps

  1. Wash and dry the lettuce, cucumbers and other vegetables with a napkin
  2. Chop the lettuce, shred the carrots, and slice the cucumbers, onion and tomatoes
  3. Put the chopped letuuce into a large bowl
  4. Next, layer with the cucumbers, carrots and onion
  5. Top this with the baked beans, which you should drain
  6. Slice the eggs, and cube the avocado
  7. Then, add these, together with your tomatoes onto the salad
  8. Finally, drizzle your salad cream onto the mixture
  9. If preferred, add ketchup, then serve
