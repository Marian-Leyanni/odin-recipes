Fruit Smoothie

Make a refreshing smoothie with this great smoothie recipe

This smoothie recipe is the perfect complement to your day.

It is chock full of fruity goodness.

Yes, you can use whatever fruit you like. Just experiment

Ingredients

  • Your favorite fruits
  • Ice
  • Orange or mango juice

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Clean fruits, then blend until smooth
  3. Add ice and mix in juice, then blend again.
  4. Bon appetit!
